# KPI Finalization

1. **Total Sales & Profit by Year**  
   **KPI**: Total revenue and profit for each year.

2. **Sales by Market (Region, Country, City)**  
   **KPI**: Break down total sales by market.

3. **Sales by Customer Segment**  
   **KPI**: How much revenue comes from different customer types (e.g., Corporate, Consumer).

4. **Top-Selling Products by Revenue & Quantity**  
   **KPI**: Which products generate the most revenue and have the highest sales volume.

5. **Unprofitable Orders (Losses)**  
   **KPI**: Orders where profit is negative.

6. **Return Rate by Market**  
   **KPI**: Percentage of returns by market (Region/Country).

7. **Customer Satisfaction (Return Rates by Product Category/Subcategory)**  
   **KPI**: Return rates for each product category/subcategory.

8. **Profit Margin by Market**  
   **KPI**: Profit margin for each market (Region, Country).

9. **Product Sales Trends (High vs. Low Volume)**  
   **KPI**: Track whether products are consistently high-sellers or have low sales.

10. **Revenue vs. Profit for Each Market**  
    **KPI**: Compare revenue and profit to understand market potential.

11. **Return Rates per Year**  
    **KPI**: Annual return rates across all markets.

12. **Success Orders by Year/Market**  
    **KPI**: Orders that were delivered successfully (i.e., not returned).

13. **Revenue vs. Loss in Each Market Annually**  
    **KPI**: Understand where losses are coming from in each market.

14. **Most Successful Product Categories/Subcategories**  
    **KPI**: Which categories/subcategories sell the most and have the highest profits.

15. **Average Order Value (AOV)**  
    **KPI**: Average value of orders for each customer segment or market.
